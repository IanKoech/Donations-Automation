Our Mission

We are a non-profit organisation that helps various charities get funding from donors and also allows donors to pledge their conrtibution. We store donor and charity details inside a database. We also provide donors with the option of anonymity. Donors whose details are saved and had previously pledged to make a donation will be reminded when to make the donation.

Our Approach

Connecting

Patnership is key to everything we do.We recognise that in order to provide young people with the best possible services and future, collaboration is key. We work with many and varied individuals and organisations to break down the artificial barriers that restrict collaborative work, so that young people have the chance to be as healthy, safe and happy as possible

Supporting

We believe in the potential of young people to make positive changes. We aim to support young people in increasing their ability, willingness and confidence to access high quality, appropriate youth work, health care and education, so that these changes can become reality.

Innovating

We aim to always innovate in delivery and integrate youth work provision and health services. We know that the problems young people face evolve and change, and our priority is to constantly consult young people about these issues and , along with our skilled patners, come up with innovative and effective ways to address them that are youth-focused, sustainable and scalable
